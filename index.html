About Us

Utilities is one of the oldest and most reputable companies in New York. Created in 1993 by Thomas Sterling, we have over 20 years of experience in plumbing and we can definitely say that there is nothing that we can’t fix.

All we have achieved is thanks to the creativity and professionalism of our staff. Individual style and warm relationships with our customers brought us much more than just a positive reputation.
